# #541 Swadloon (Leaf-Wrapped Pokémon)

**Rising Ruby:** Forests where Swadloon live have superb foliage because the nutrients they make from fallen leaves nourish the plant life.

**Sinking Sapphire:** It protects itself from the cold by wrapping up in leaves. It stays on the move, eating leaves in forests.
