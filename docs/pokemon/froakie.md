# #656 Froakie (Bubble Frog Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/froakie/official.png "Froakie") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/froakie/official_shiny.png "Froakie") |

**Rising Ruby:** It secretes flexible bubbles from its chest and back. The bubbles reduce the damage it would otherwise take when attacked.

**Sinking Sapphire:** It protects its skin by covering its body in delicate bubbles. Beneath its happy-go-lucky air, it keeps a watchful eye on its surroundings.
