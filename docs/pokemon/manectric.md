# #310 Manectric (Discharge Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/manectric/official.png "Manectric") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/manectric/official_shiny.png "Manectric") |

**Rising Ruby:** Manectric is constantly discharging electricity from its mane. The sparks sometimes ignite forest fires. When it enters a battle, this Pokémon creates thunderclouds.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Manectric discharges strong electricity from its mane. The mane is used for collecting electricity in the atmosphere. This Pokémon creates thunderclouds above its head.
