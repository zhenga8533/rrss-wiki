# #584 Vanilluxe (Snowstorm Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/vanilluxe/official.png "Vanilluxe") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/vanilluxe/official_shiny.png "Vanilluxe") |

**Rising Ruby:** If both heads get angry simultaneously, this Pokémon expels a blizzard, burying everything in snow.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Swallowing large amounts of water, they make snow clouds inside their bodies and, when angry, cause violent blizzards.
