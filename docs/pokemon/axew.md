**Rising Ruby:** They use their tusks to crush the berries they eat. Repeated regrowth makes their tusks strong and sharp.

**Sinking Sapphire:** They mark their territory by leaving gashes in trees with their tusks. If a tusk breaks, a new one grows in quickly.
