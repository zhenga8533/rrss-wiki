# #260 Swampert (Mud Fish Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/swampert/official.png "Swampert") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/swampert/official_shiny.png "Swampert") |

**Rising Ruby:** Swampert is very strong. It has enough power to easily drag a boulder weighing more than a ton. This Pokémon also has powerful vision that lets it see even in murky water.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Swampert predicts storms by sensing subtle differences in the sounds of waves and tidal winds with its fins. If a storm is approaching, it piles up boulders to protect itself.
