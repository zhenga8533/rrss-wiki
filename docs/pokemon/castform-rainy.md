# Castform Rainy (Weather Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/castform-rainy/official.png "Castform Rainy") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/castform-rainy/official_shiny.png "Castform Rainy") |

**Rising Ruby:** Castform’s appearance changes with the weather. This Pokémon gained the ability to use the vast power of nature to protect its tiny body.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Castform borrows the power of nature to transform itself into the guises of the sun, rain, and snow-clouds. This Pokémon’s feelings change with the weather.
