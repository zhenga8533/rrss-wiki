# #537 Seismitoad (Vibration Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/seismitoad/official.png "Seismitoad") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/seismitoad/official_shiny.png "Seismitoad") |

**Rising Ruby:** It increases the power of its punches by vibrating the bumps on its fists. It can turn a boulder to rubble with one punch.

**Sinking Sapphire:** They shoot paralyzing liquid from their head bumps. They use vibration to hurt their opponents.
