# Hoopa Unbound (Mischief Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/hoopa-unbound/official.png "Hoopa Unbound") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/hoopa-unbound/official_shiny.png "Hoopa Unbound") |

**Rising Ruby:** In its true form, it possess a huge amount of power. Legends of its avarice tell how it once carried off an entire castle to gain the treasure hidden within.

**Sinking Sapphire:** It is said to be able to seize anything it desires with its six rings and six huge arms. With its power sealed, it is transformed into a much smaller form.
