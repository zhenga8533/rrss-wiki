# #116 Horsea (Dragon Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/horsea/official.png "Horsea") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/horsea/official_shiny.png "Horsea") |

**Rising Ruby:** Horsea eats small insects and moss off of rocks. If the ocean current turns fast, this Pokémon anchors itself by wrapping its tail around rocks or coral to prevent being washed away.

**Sinking Sapphire:** If Horsea senses danger, it will reflexively spray a dense black ink from its mouth and try to escape. This Pokémon swims by cleverly flapping the fin on its back.
