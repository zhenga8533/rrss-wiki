# #306 Aggron (Iron Armor Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/aggron/official.png "Aggron") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/aggron/official_shiny.png "Aggron") |

**Rising Ruby:** Aggron claims an entire mountain as its own territory. It mercilessly beats up anything that violates its environment. This Pokémon vigilantly patrols its territory at all times.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Aggron is protective of its environment. If its mountain is ravaged by a landslide or a fire, this Pokémon will haul topsoil to the area, plant trees, and beautifully restore its own territory.
