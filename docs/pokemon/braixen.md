# #654 Braixen (Fox Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/braixen/official.png "Braixen") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/braixen/official_shiny.png "Braixen") |

**Rising Ruby:** It has a twig stuck in its tail. With friction from its tail fur, it sets the twig on fire and launches into battle.

**Sinking Sapphire:** When the twig is plucked from its tail, friction sets the twig alight. The flame is used to send signals to its allies.
