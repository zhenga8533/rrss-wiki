# #230 Kingdra (Dragon Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/kingdra/official.png "Kingdra") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/kingdra/official_shiny.png "Kingdra") |

**Rising Ruby:** Kingdra lives at extreme ocean depths that are otherwise uninhabited. It has long been believed that the yawning of this Pokémon creates spiraling ocean currents.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Kingdra sleeps on the seafloor where it is otherwise devoid of life. When a storm arrives, the Pokémon is said to awaken and wander about in search of prey.
