# #325 Spoink (Bounce Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/spoink/official.png "Spoink") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/spoink/official_shiny.png "Spoink") |

**Rising Ruby:** Spoink bounces around on its tail. The shock of its bouncing makes its heart pump. As a result, this Pokémon cannot afford to stop bouncing—if it stops, its heart will stop.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Spoink keeps a pearl on top of its head. The pearl functions to amplify this Pokémon’s psychokinetic powers. It is therefore on a constant search for a bigger pearl.
