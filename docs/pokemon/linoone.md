# #264 Linoone (Rushing Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/linoone/official.png "Linoone") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/linoone/official_shiny.png "Linoone") |

**Rising Ruby:** Linoone always runs full speed and only in straight lines. If facing an obstacle, it makes a right-angle turn to evade it. This Pokémon is very challenged by gently curving roads.

**Sinking Sapphire:** When hunting, Linoone will make a beeline straight for the prey at a full run. While this Pokémon is capable of topping 60 mph, it has to come to a screeching halt before it can turn.
