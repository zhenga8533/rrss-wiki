# #617 Accelgor (Shell Out Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/accelgor/official.png "Accelgor") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/accelgor/official_shiny.png "Accelgor") |

**Rising Ruby:** Having removed its heavy shell, it becomes very light and can fight with ninja-like movements.

**Sinking Sapphire:** When its body dries out, it weakens. So, to prevent dehydration, it wraps itself in many layers of thin membrane.
