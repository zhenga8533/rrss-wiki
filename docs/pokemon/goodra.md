**Rising Ruby:** This very friendly Dragon-type Pokémon will hug its beloved Trainer, leaving that Trainer covered in sticky slime.

**Sinking Sapphire:** It attacks with retractable horns. It throws a punch that’s the equivalent of the force of a hundred pro boxers.
