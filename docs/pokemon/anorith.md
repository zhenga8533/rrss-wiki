# #347 Anorith (Old Shrimp Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/anorith/official.png "Anorith") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/anorith/official_shiny.png "Anorith") |

**Rising Ruby:** Anorith was regenerated from a prehistoric fossil. This primitive Pokémon once lived in warm seas. It grips its prey firmly between its two large claws.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Anorith is said to be a type of Pokémon predecessor, with eight wings at the sides of its body. This Pokémon swam in the primordial sea by undulating these eight wings.
