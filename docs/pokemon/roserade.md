# #407 Roserade (Bouquet Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/roserade/official.png "Roserade") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/roserade/official_shiny.png "Roserade") |

**Rising Ruby:** Luring prey with a sweet scent, it uses poison whips on its arms to poison, bind, and finish off the prey.

**Sinking Sapphire:** With the movements of a dancer, it strikes with whips that are densely lined with poison thorns.
