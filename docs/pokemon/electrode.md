# #101 Electrode (Ball Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/electrode/official.png "Electrode") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/electrode/official_shiny.png "Electrode") |

**Rising Ruby:** Electrode eats electricity in the atmosphere. On days when lightning strikes, you can see this Pokémon exploding all over the place from eating too much electricity.

**Sinking Sapphire:** One of Electrode’s characteristics is its attraction to electricity. It is a problematical Pokémon that congregates mostly at electrical power plants to feed on electricity that has just been generated.
