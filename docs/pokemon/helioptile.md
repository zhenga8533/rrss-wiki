# #694 Helioptile (Generator Pokémon)

**Rising Ruby:** They make their home in deserts. They can generate their energy from basking in the sun, so eating food is not a requirement.

**Sinking Sapphire:** The frills on either side of its head have cells that generate electricity when exposed to sunlight.
