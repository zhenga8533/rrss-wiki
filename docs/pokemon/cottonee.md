# #546 Cottonee (Cotton Puff Pokémon)

**Rising Ruby:** When attacked, it escapes by shooting cotton from its body. The cotton serves as a decoy to distract the attacker.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Perhaps because they feel more at ease in a group, they stick to others they find. They end up looking like a cloud.
