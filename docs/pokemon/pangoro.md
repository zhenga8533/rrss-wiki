# #675 Pangoro (Daunting Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/pangoro/official.png "Pangoro") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/pangoro/official_shiny.png "Pangoro") |

**Rising Ruby:** Although it possesses a violent temperament, it won’t put up with bullying. It uses the leaf in its mouth to sense the movements of its enemies.

**Sinking Sapphire:** It charges ahead and bashes its opponents like a berserker, uncaring about any hits it might take. Its arms are mighty enough to snap a telephone pole.
