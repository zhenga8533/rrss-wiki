# #065 Alakazam (Psi Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/alakazam/official.png "Alakazam") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/alakazam/official_shiny.png "Alakazam") |

**Rising Ruby:** Alakazam’s brain continually grows, making its head far too heavy to support with its neck. This Pokémon holds its head up using its psychokinetic power instead.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Alakazam’s brain continually grows, infinitely multiplying brain cells. This amazing brain gives this Pokémon an astoundingly high IQ of 5,000. It has a thorough memory of everything that has occurred in the world.
