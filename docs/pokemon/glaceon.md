# #471 Glaceon (Fresh Snow Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/glaceon/official.png "Glaceon") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/glaceon/official_shiny.png "Glaceon") |

**Rising Ruby:** By controlling its body heat, it can freeze the atmosphere around it to make a diamond-dust flurry.

**Sinking Sapphire:** It lowers its body heat to freeze its fur. The hairs then become like needles it can fire.
