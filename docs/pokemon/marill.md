# #183 Marill (Aqua Mouse Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/marill/official.png "Marill") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/marill/official_shiny.png "Marill") |

**Rising Ruby:** Marill’s oil-filled tail acts much like a life preserver. If you see just its tail bobbing on the water’s surface, it’s a sure indication that this Pokémon is diving beneath the water to feed on aquatic plants.

**Sinking Sapphire:** When fishing for food at the edge of a fast-running stream, Marill wraps its tail around the trunk of a tree. This Pokémon’s tail is flexible and configured to stretch.
