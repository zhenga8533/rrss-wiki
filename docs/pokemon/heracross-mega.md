# Heracross Mega (Single Horn Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/heracross-mega/official.png "Heracross Mega") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/heracross-mega/official_shiny.png "Heracross Mega") |

**Rising Ruby:** Heracross charges in a straight line at its foe, slips beneath the foe’s grasp, and then scoops up and hurls the opponent with its mighty horn. This Pokémon even has enough power to topple a massive tree.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Heracross has sharp claws on its feet. These are planted firmly into the ground or the bark of a tree, giving the Pokémon a secure and solid footing to forcefully fling away foes with its proud horn.
