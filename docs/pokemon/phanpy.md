# #231 Phanpy (Long Nose Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/phanpy/official.png "Phanpy") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/phanpy/official_shiny.png "Phanpy") |

**Rising Ruby:** For its nest, Phanpy digs a vertical pit in the ground at the edge of a river. It marks the area around its nest with its trunk to let the others know that the area has been claimed.

**Sinking Sapphire:** Phanpy uses its long nose to shower itself. When others gather around, they thoroughly douse each other with water. These Pokémon can be seen drying their soaking-wet bodies at the edge of water.
